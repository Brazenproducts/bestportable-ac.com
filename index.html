What to Look For in a Portable AC

Choosing the right portable AC comes down to a few key factors: BTU rating for your room size, noise level, energy efficiency, and whether you want single-hose or dual-hose. Our complete buyer's guide breaks down everything you need to know, but here's the quick version:

  • Small rooms (up to 300 sq ft): 8,000 BTU is plenty
  • Medium rooms (300–450 sq ft): Look for 10,000–12,000 BTU
  • Large rooms (450+ sq ft): You'll want 14,000 BTU
